# Billing & Plans

Everything you need to know about Celune's pricing tiers, usage limits, and subscription management.

---

| ------------------------ | ------------ | ------ | --------- | ---------- |

All paid plans are billed monthly. Annual billing is available on Pro and Team — [contact us](mailto:hello@celune.ai) for pricing.

## Free Tier (Spark)

The Spark plan is designed for individuals exploring what Celune can do. No credit card is required to get started.

**What's included:**

- 2 agents
- 1 workspace
- 1 member seat
- 1,000 memory entries
- Full access to the dashboard, settings, and API

**Limitations:**

- Voice mode is not available on Spark
- Memory is capped at 100 entries — oldest entries are not automatically removed, but new entries are blocked once the limit is reached
- Integrations that require team features (multi-workspace routing, shared agents) are not available

> You can upgrade to Pro at any time from **Settings → Billing**. No data is lost when upgrading.

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade, go to **Settings → Billing → Choose Plan** in your Celune dashboard. Select the plan you want and confirm your payment method.

Upgrades take effect immediately. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period.

**Steps:**

1. Open your Celune dashboard
2. Navigate to **Settings → Billing**
3. Click **Choose Plan**
4. Select your desired tier
5. Enter your payment details and confirm

> Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You will retain access to your current plan's features until then.

## Managing Your Subscription

All subscription management is available in **Settings → Billing**.

**View invoices** — Past invoices are listed under the Billing History section. Each invoice links to a downloadable PDF.

**Update payment method** — Click **Update Payment Method** to replace your card or billing details. Changes apply to the next billing cycle.

**Cancel your subscription** — Click **Cancel Plan** to cancel at the end of your current period. Your workspace and data are retained. You will be downgraded to Spark automatically when the period ends.

> Cancellation does not delete your data. You can reactivate at any time by choosing a plan in **Settings → Billing**.

## Usage Limits

When you reach a plan limit, Celune blocks the action rather than charging overage fees. Here is what happens for each limit type:

| Limit          | What Happens When You Hit It                                                           |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Agents**     | You cannot create a new agent until you delete an existing one or upgrade your plan    |
| **Workspaces** | Creating a new workspace is blocked. Existing workspaces and their data are unaffected |
| **Members**    | Inviting a new member is blocked. Existing members retain full access                  |
| **Memory**     | New memory entries are blocked. Existing entries remain intact and searchable          |

You can check current usage at any time in **Settings → Billing → Usage**.

> If you are approaching a limit, Celune will display a warning banner in the dashboard. No action is required until you actually hit the limit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Celune prorate upgrades?**
Yes. When you upgrade mid-cycle, you are charged only for the remaining days in your billing period at the new plan rate. The adjustment appears on your next invoice.

**Are there refunds?**
Celune does not offer refunds on monthly subscriptions. If you cancel, you retain access until the end of your paid period. For annual plans, contact [hello@celune.ai](mailto:hello@celune.ai) within 14 days of purchase if you have concerns.

**Is annual billing available?**
Annual billing is available on Pro and Team plans and includes a discount compared to monthly pricing. To switch to annual, contact [hello@celune.ai](mailto:hello@celune.ai) or look for the toggle in **Settings → Billing** when it becomes available in your region.

**What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
Your data is never deleted on downgrade. If your usage exceeds the lower plan's limits (for example, you have 8 agents on Team and downgrade to Pro), you will be prompted to reduce usage to within the new limits before the downgrade takes effect.

**Can I change plans more than once per month?**
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade as often as needed. Each change is prorated against your current billing cycle.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Celune accepts all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can arrange invoicing and PO-based payments by contacting [hello@celune.ai](mailto:hello@celune.ai).

**Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
There is no time-limited free trial. The Spark plan is free indefinitely and gives you a feel for the platform. Upgrade when you are ready to scale.
